    About TMU Rocketry

    TMU Rocketry is a student-led aerospace engineering organization dedicated to advancing rocket propulsion technology and avionics systems. We specialize in the design, development, and testing of liquid propulsion systems, integrated with sophisticated flight computers and ground station software.

    Our comprehensive research program encompasses circuit design for mission-critical avionics, precision data acquisition during engine testing, and mission control software for high-altitude flight operations. Through rigorous testing protocols and detailed technical documentation, we provide students with professional aerospace engineering experience.
